Urban Air Mobility Research In Virtual Reality With CAVE System

NASA-funded project utilizing Unity 3D to simulate urban air mobility in a virtual San Francisco Bay Area, assessing safety and efficiency.


Developed a pedal-operated air vehicle with yaw, pitch, and roll control. The pilot uses a Logitech Attack 3 joystick to perform pitch and roll maneuvers, and uses the pedals from a Logitech G920 driving wheel to perform yaw.


Conducted evaluation of Mapbox and Google 3D Tiles as potential alternatives for city generation API.


Currently Work in Progress

Vertical situation display on the cockpit to provide pilots visuals on how to approach vertiports.
